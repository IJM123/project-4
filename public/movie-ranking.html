The Best Bollywood Movies According to Insiya

My top 3 movies of all-time

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobrara

This is one of my favorite bollywood movies because it consists of
love, comedy, adventure, friends, and family all in one. I've watched it
hundreds of times and can never get tired of it.

3 idiots

This movie is a full comedy movie and it also has important lessons.
It is just a great watch overall.

Yeh Jawaani Heh Deewaani

This one has been a popular one for a long time but I had just seen it
couple of months ago and let me tell you, it is a little similar to ZNMD
but also has its own story and I absolutley love it.
